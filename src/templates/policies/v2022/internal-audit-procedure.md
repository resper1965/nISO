# [Organization Name] - Internal Audit Procedure
**Document ID:** PROC-AUD-001 | **Classification:** Internal | **Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Objective (ISO 27001 Cl. 9.2)
To define the process for planning and conducting internal audits to ensure the ISMS/PIMS remains compliant and effective.

## 2. Audit Frequency
- The entire scope of the ISMS must be audited at least once per year.
- High-risk areas may be audited more frequently.

## 3. Auditor Independence
- Internal auditors must be independent of the function being audited to ensure objectivity.

## 4. Audit Phases
1. **Planning:** Define scope, criteria, and schedule.
2. **Execution:** Collect evidence (interviews, documents, technical checks).
3. **Reporting:** Document non-conformities (NC) and improvement opportunities.
4. **Follow-up:** Verify the effectiveness of corrective actions.
