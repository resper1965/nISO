# POLICY 4: Supplier and Third-Party Security Policy

## 1. Purpose
Define security requirements for all vendors and service providers of [Organization Name].

## 2. Supplier Tiers
- **Tier 1:** Critical/High Risk (Requires annual audit).
- **Tier 2:** Medium Risk (Self-assessment).
- **Tier 3:** Low Risk.

## 3. Policy Statements
- All Tier 1 suppliers must sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).
- Preference shall be given to ISO 27001 or SOC 2 certified vendors.
