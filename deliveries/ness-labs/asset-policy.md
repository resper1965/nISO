# POLICY 3: Asset Management Policy

## 1. Purpose
Ensures protection of ness. labs assets and defines acceptable use.

## 2. Classification
- **Public:** No restrictions.
- **Internal:** Restricted to employees.
- **Confidential:** Specific teams only.
- **Restricted:** High sensitivity (Legal/Financial).

## 3. Policy Statements
- Every asset must have a designated owner.
- Return of assets is mandatory upon termination of contract/employment.

## 4. Roles & Responsibilities
- **Owner:** Ricardo Resper (CISO)
- **Approver:** Management Board
