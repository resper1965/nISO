# ness. labs - Privacy Information Management System (PIMS) Policy
**Document ID:** POL-PIMS-001 | **Classification:** Public/Internal | **Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Objective (ISO 27701 Cl. 5.2)
This policy establishes the framework for managing Personal Identifiable Information (PII) at ness. labs, extending the ISMS to include privacy protection as per ISO 27701:2019.

## 2. Scope (ISO 27701 Cl. 4.3)
Applies to all PII processing activities where ness. labs acts as a PII Controller or PII Processor.

## 3. PII Processing Principles
### 3.1 Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency
- PII must be processed based on a valid legal ground (Consent, Legal Obligation, etc.).
- Processing purposes must be clearly communicated to data subjects.

### 3.2 Purpose Limitation and Data Minimization
- Data collection is restricted to what is strictly necessary for the defined purpose.

## 4. Data Subject Rights
- ness. labs provides mechanisms for data subjects to exercise their rights (access, rectification, erasure, objection).

## 5. Roles and Responsibilities
- **Data Protection Officer (DPO):** Oversight of PIMS compliance and liaison with regulators.
- **PII Processors:** Compliance with security and privacy instructions.

## 6. Monitoring and Measurement
PII processing activities are audited periodically via the Data Processing Inventory (RoPA).
