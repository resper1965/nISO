# POLICY 2: Risk Assessment and Treatment Policy

## 1. Purpose
Define the methodology for identifying, analyzing, and treating information security risks at ness. labs.

## 2. Methodology
- **Scale:** 1-25 (Impact x Likelihood).
- **Treatment Options:** Mitigate, Accept, Transfer, Avoid.

## 3. Policy Statements
- All critical assets must undergo a formal risk assessment annually.
- The Statement of Applicability (SoA) must be updated after each assessment.

## 4. Roles & Responsibilities
- **Owner:** Ricardo Resper (CISO)
- **Approver:** Management Board
