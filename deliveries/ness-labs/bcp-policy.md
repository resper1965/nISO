# POLICY 5: Business Continuity and Incident Response Policy

## 1. Purpose
Framework for responding to security incidents and ensuring continuity of operations at ness. labs.

## 2. Targets
- **RTO (Recovery Time Objective):** Maximum allowable downtime.
- **RPO (Recovery Point Objective):** Maximum allowable data loss.

## 3. Incident Response Phases
1. Preparation
2. Identification
3. Containment
4. Eradication
5. Recovery
6. Lessons Learned

## 4. Policy Statements
- All security incidents must be reported via [Incident Channel].
- BCP/DRP plans must be tested at least annually.

- **Approver:** Management Board
